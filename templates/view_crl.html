About CRLs

A Certificate Revocation List (CRL) contains all certificates that have been revoked by this Certificate Authority and should no longer be trusted.

Common Revocation Reasons
  • Key Compromise: Private key exposed
  • Superseded: Certificate replaced
  • Cessation: No longer needed
  • Unspecified: General revocation
CRLs should be updated regularly and distributed to all systems that need to verify certificates.
