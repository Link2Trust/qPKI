Welcome to qPKI
Quantum-Safe Public Key Infrastructure

qPKI provides a comprehensive solution for managing hybrid certificates that combine classical cryptography (RSA or ECC) with post-quantum cryptography (Dilithium). This approach ensures your certificates remain secure even against quantum computer attacks.

Supported Algorithms
Classical Algorithms:
  • RSA (2048, 3072, 4096 bits)
  • ECC (P-256, P-384, P-521)
Post-Quantum Algorithms:
  • Dilithium2 (128-bit security)
  • Dilithium3 (192-bit security)
  • Dilithium5 (256-bit security)
